OUR MEMBERS

SALTINE

Saltine plays guitar and bass for Pythia. She is one of the band's founders.

SUBIN

Subin plays drums and does other kinds of percussion for Pythia. She is one of the band's founders.

TAMYSKA

Tamyska plays keys and the saxophone for Pythia. He writes the jazzier and calmer songs.

TONABRIX

Tonabrix does all vocals and writes most lyrics for the songs. He brings all the songs together.

WITCHDOC

Witchdoc organizes all the albums that Pythia has ever put out.
